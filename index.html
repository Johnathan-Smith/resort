Who is Santa Clause?

Santa is that jolly old elf who makes the rounds each year, breaking and entering homes all over the world to ensure each child gets at least one Christmas present they will actually like.

What does Santa Do After Christmas?

After eating close to 7 billion cookies and drinking over a billion gallons of milk, Santa needs a little break and a lot of exercise. He spends the first three months of every year on his own private island recuperating with his reindeer.

Where is Santa's Secret Island?

Santa's secret island is located in the South pacific, not too far off the coast of Antarctica. January through March is the sunny season and boasts relatively mild temperatures.
